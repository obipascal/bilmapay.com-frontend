About

Bilma Pay is commerce payment app. What do I mean by commerce payment App? It means that everything that has to do with Paying for goods and services online, Paying for your Bills and sending funds to recognized institution. We focuses commercial payments such as Bills, Market product and transport systems.

So, how does it works? Have you ever got stuck online when paying for a product that you didn't plan for but you need it so urgent that you wish you had your bank card with you? Yes I have some times I wish I have my funds in the cloud rather than in the bank Hahaha!

Why not use Google Pay or even Apple Pay why use Bilma Pay are there not the same thing. Well I can't argue with you Bilma Pay is not different from this renounce tech giants but this solutions were not build for you. When I mean not build for you Google Pay and Apple Pay currency are by default set to Dollar ($) which means a normal Nigeria like you can not Pay-in that wallet considering the new CBN policy. This is were Bilma Pay come in to play, we built Bilma Pay with you in mind we know your pain and troubles when it comes to online or rather commercial payments online. 

Bilma Pay is a reloadable wallet the enables you to keep your funds int he cloud and request for them anytime with just a secure PIN. Yes a secure PIN, in the previous scenario above all you would have done with Bilma Pay is just Tap-and-Pay top on Pay with Bilma Pay then enter your PIN boom! The funds are sent to the vendor no need for card. Same implies with Bill payments and other services online.
